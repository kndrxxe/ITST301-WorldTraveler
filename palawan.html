PALAWAN

Palawan is an island province in the Philippines that is known for its natural beauty and rich biodiversity. Located in the western part of the country, it is composed of a main island and several smaller islands, and is bordered by the South China Sea to the west and the Sulu Sea to the east. The province is home to a number of protected areas, including the Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Palawan is also a popular destination for tourists, who come to enjoy its beautiful beaches, forests, and marine life. The capital of the province is Puerto Princesa City.

ITINERARY

Palawan is a popular destination for tourists due to its stunning natural beauty and rich biodiversity. This is just one example of a tour itinerary for Palawan, and there are many other things to see and do in the province:

DAY 1

  • Arrive in Puerto Princesa and check into your hotel.
  • Spend the afternoon exploring the city and its attractions, such as the Crocodile Farm, the Palawan Wildlife Rescue and Conservation Center, and the Iwahig Penal Colony.
  • In the evening, enjoy a delicious meal at one of the city's many restaurants.

DAY 2

  • Take a tour of the Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park. This UNESCO World Heritage Site is home to a stunning underground river that runs through a beautiful cave system.
  • After the tour, enjoy a delicious lunch at a local restaurant.

DAY 3

  • Head to El Nido, located on the northern tip of Palawan. Spend the day exploring the beautiful beaches and crystal clear waters.
  • In the evening, enjoy a sunset dinner at one of the local restaurants.

DAY 4

  • Take a tour of Coron, located on the eastern side of Palawan. This beautiful area is known for its stunning limestone cliffs and clear waters, and is a popular destination for diving and snorkeling.
  • In the evening, enjoy a delicious meal at a local restaurant.

DAY 5

  • Spend the day exploring the Tubbataha Reefs Natural Park, a protected area in the Sulu Sea that is home to a wide variety of marine life.
  • In the evening, return to your hotel in Puerto Princesa for a relaxing night.

DAY 6

  • Spend the day exploring Honda Bay, located near Puerto Princesa City. This beautiful area is known for its beautiful beaches and clear waters, and is a popular spot for island hopping.
  • In the evening, enjoy a farewell dinner at a local restaurant.

DAY 7

  • Depart Palawan and head back home.
