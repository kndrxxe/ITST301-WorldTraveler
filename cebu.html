CEBU

Cebu is an island province located in the Central Visayas region of the Philippines. It is the main hub of the Visayas and one of the most populous cities in the country. Located in the eastern part of the Visayas region, Cebu is known for its beautiful beaches, historic landmarks, and cultural attractions. It is also a major economic and transportation hub, with a busy port and an international airport. The province is home to a diverse population, with a mix of indigenous peoples, Spanish colonial influences, and other cultural influences. Cebu is also known for its delicious cuisine, which is a blend of Filipino, Chinese, and Spanish flavors.

ITINERARY

Cebu is known for its beautiful beaches, historic landmarks, and cultural attractions, it is a vibrant and lively place, with a rich history and culture that is reflected in its landmarks, festivals, and traditions. Here is a sample itinerary for a tour of Cebu:

DAY 1

  • Arrival in Cebu
  • Check-in at your hotel
  • Visit Magellan's Cross and Fort San Pedro
  • Explore Colon Street, the oldest street in the Philippines
  • Enjoy dinner at a local restaurant

DAY 2

  • Visit the Taoist Cemetery, a unique cemetery with ornately decorated tombs
  • Visit Taoist temples in the area
  • Head to Mactan Island and visit the Mactan Island Aquarium
  • Enjoy a beach day at Mactan Island

DAY 3

  • Take a day trip to the Osmeña Peak, the highest peak in Cebu
  • Visit the Taoist Hail Stone House, a house made entirely of hailstones
  • Visit the Taoist Crystal Mountain, a mountain made entirely of crystal
  • Enjoy dinner at a local restaurant

DAY 4

  • Visit the Taoist Temple in the Hills, a beautiful temple located in the hills surrounding Cebu
  • Take a boat ride to the nearby island of Bohol and visit the Chocolate Hills
  • Visit the Tarsier Sanctuary and see the world's smallest primate
  • Return to Cebu and enjoy dinner at a local restaurant

DAY 5

  • Depart Cebu
